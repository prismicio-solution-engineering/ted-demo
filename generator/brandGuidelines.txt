website description:
website of a company organizing talks from expert speakers on education, business, science, tech and creativity, with subtitles in 100+ languages.

brand guidelines:
- no border radius
- light drop shadow
- main color: #eb0028 black and white
- text color: #555555\n- background color: white or #ffefa1 sometimes
- Font: Inter
- Padding is consistent and everywhere, even in buttons, it's of 12px between horizontal elements and there is always a minimum of 40px on the sides of the sections.
- Texts, buttons are always left-aligned on the page and in sections
- Elements are evenly distributed across the width of the section
- dropdowns buttons have a visible default value.
